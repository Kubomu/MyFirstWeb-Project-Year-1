Frequently Asked Questions

What is the mission of Hoops Basketball Team?

The mission of Hoops Basketball Team is to promote the sport of basketball and foster a positive, inclusive environment for players of all ages and abilities. We aim to provide a fun, competitive experience for our players and create lasting memories for our fans.

Where can I buy Hoops Basketball Team merchandise?

You can buy Hoops Basketball Team merchandise on our website or at our games. We offer a variety of apparel, accessories, and other items that showcase your support for the team.

How can I become a sponsor of Hoops Basketball Team?

If you are interested in becoming a sponsor of Hoops Basketball Team, please contact us at [sponsorship@hoopsbasketballteam.com](mailto:sponsorship@hoopsbasketballteam.com) for more information. We offer various sponsorship packages that can help promote your brand and support our team.

How can I get tickets to Hoops Basketball Team games?

You can purchase tickets to Hoops Basketball Team games on our website or at the box office. We offer a variety of seating options and ticket packages to fit your needs.

Where can I find the schedule for Hoops Basketball Team games?

You can find the schedule for Hoops Basketball Team games on our website. We regularly update our schedule to reflect any changes or additions, so be sure to check back often.

Directions to Home Court

Our home court is located at 123 Main Street, Anytown, USA. Here are directions to get there:

  1. From the north, take I-95 South to Exit 10.
  2. Turn right onto Main Street and continue for 2 miles.
  3. The home court will be on your left, just past the high school.
